## Professional VIO Aesthetic ✅

### Design Principles Applied:

1. **Black Dominance (70%)**
   - Primary buttons
   - Icons
   - Headings on white
   - Hero background

2. **White Space (25%)**
   - Card backgrounds
   - Text on black
   - Clean sections

3. **Yellow Restraint (5%)**
   - Thin borders only (1-3px)
   - Strategic accents
   - Never for large areas

4. **Gray Hierarchy**
   - Section labels
   - Subtle text
   - Professional tone
